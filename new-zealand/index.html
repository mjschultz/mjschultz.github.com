New Zealand

Here is a video summary of Lyndsie and my trip to New Zealand. In some cases the photos provide a cleaner version, in others video does more justice. I am sorry about the shaky-ness in some of the shots, but the camera is a simple Flip UltraHD (but I still think it did an excellent job).

This includes shots from Auckland with Sky Tower, Queenstown with Bob’s Peak, our trip to the Milford Sound, the TranzAlpine Tour, Botanical Gardens in Christchurch, Kaikoura (with Dusky Dolphins), and Blenheim.
